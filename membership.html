Common questions

Before you join

Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier?

Yes — anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What happens right after I pay?

You will receive a welcome email within minutes with your login and first week of content. Circle invitation (Ember and above) follows within 24 hours.

Do I need to be religious to participate?

No. Open Faith means your beliefs — or absence of them — are welcome here. Dignity is the only non-negotiable.

What is a Circle?

A weekly small group of 6–10 people facilitated by a trained C.O.R.E Steward. Circles meet online and follow a consistent ritual structure. Included in Ember and above.

What is the transformation track?

An eight-week evidence-based program tied directly to A.L. Lindsey's book. Structured exercises, reflection tools, and coaching check-ins. Included in Diamond and above.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All memberships are month-to-month. Cancel and retain access through the end of your billing period.
