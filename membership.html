Common Questions

Is The Round a church?

No. The Round is a practice-based spiritual community with Open Faith — meaning people of all beliefs (or no belief) are welcome. We never pressure belief or hide it.

Is this therapy?

No. Circles are not clinical treatment. If you need professional mental health support, we encourage you to seek it. C.O.R.E is structured practice with trained facilitation — not therapy.

Can I change tiers later?

Yes. You can move between tiers at any time. Start where you are.

What is a Circle?

A Circle is a weekly small group (6–10 people) led by a trained C.O.R.E Steward. It follows a simple ritual: arrive, ground, share, revise, commit, close. Available at Ember tier and above.

What is a Steward?

A C.O.R.E Steward is a certified facilitator who leads Circles within The Round.
