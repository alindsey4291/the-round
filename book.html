The Book

The philosophy behind The Round

A book built around a simple philosophy: as long as you're alive, there is room. Room to learn. Room to repair. Room to reinterpret. Room to begin again without calling it failure.

Every chapter includes Field Notes and exercises, because ideas should be lived, not admired. Philosophy is only useful if it shows up when you're tired, when you're triggered, when you're tempted to give up on yourself.
