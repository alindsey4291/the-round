Common questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier?

Yes — anytime from your member dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What happens right after I pay?

You will receive a welcome email within minutes with your portal login and first week of tier-specific content. Your Circle invitation (Ember and above) follows within 24 hours.

Do I need to be religious?

No. Open Faith means your beliefs — or absence of them — are welcome here. Devout, doubtful, or somewhere in between. Dignity is the only non-negotiable.

What is a Circle?

A weekly small group (6–10 people) facilitated by a trained C.O.R.E Steward. Circles meet online and follow a consistent ritual structure. Included in Ember and above.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All memberships are month-to-month unless you choose annual. Cancel from your member dashboard and retain access through the end of your billing period.
