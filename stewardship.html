Stewardship

A C.O.R.E Steward doesn't lead by having the best answers. You lead by protecting the conditions where people can change.

What is a Steward?

Facilitation as leadership

C.O.R.E Stewards are certified facilitators who lead Circles for C.O.R.E Companions within The Round. Your tone becomes the nervous system of the Circle.

Stewards guide Circles back to purpose without shaming. The core tools are reflection, questions, timekeeping, and respectful redirection. Silence isn't failure — it's often integration.

Your job is to keep the space safe enough that no one has to perform. You protect process, not outcomes. Consent and confidentiality are non-negotiable.

Certification

Practicum & Assessment

Certification demonstrates you can run a Circle that is consistent, safe, inclusive, and spiritually grounded. You will lead practice Circles, receive feedback, revise your approach, and prove you can protect the container when emotions rise. The goal isn't perfection — it's competency plus humility.
