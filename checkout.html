Common questions

Before you join

Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier?

Yes — anytime from your member dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What happens right after I pay?

You will receive a welcome email within minutes with your portal login and your first week of content. Your Circle invitation (Ember and above) follows within 24 hours.

Do I need to be religious to participate?

No. Open Faith means your beliefs — or absence of them — are welcome here. Devout, doubtful, or somewhere in between. Dignity is the only non-negotiable.

What is a Circle?

A weekly small group of 6–10 people facilitated by a trained C.O.R.E Steward. Circles meet online and follow a consistent ritual structure. Included in Ember and above.

What is the transformation track?

An eight-week evidence-based program tied directly to the chapters in A.L. Lindsey's book. Each week builds on C.O.R.E practice with structured exercises, reflection tools, and coaching check-ins. Included in Diamond and above.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All memberships are month-to-month. Cancel from your member dashboard and retain access through the end of your billing period.

How is Stewardship different from regular membership?

Stewardship is a leadership track. You receive everything in Diamond plus an eight-module certification curriculum, Steward Council membership, a Circle kit license, and the right to officially facilitate Circles within The Round.
