Common questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier later?

Yes. You can change your tier at any time from your member dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What happens right after I pay?

You will receive a welcome email within minutes with your portal login and your first week of tier-specific content. Your Circle invitation (for Ember and above) will follow within 24 hours.

Do I need to be religious to participate?

No. Open Faith means exactly that — your beliefs, or absence of them, are welcome here. The Round includes people who are devout, people who are doubtful, and people who are somewhere in between. Dignity is the only non-negotiable.

What is a Circle and how do I join one?

A Circle is a weekly small group (6–10 people) facilitated by a trained C.O.R.E Steward. Circles meet online and follow a consistent ritual structure. Ember tier and above includes Circle access. You will be matched and invited after joining.

How is Stewardship different from regular membership?

Stewardship is a leadership track. You receive everything in Diamond plus an eight-module certification curriculum, access to the Steward Council, a Circle kit license, and the right to officially facilitate Circles within The Round.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All memberships are month-to-month unless you choose annual billing. You can cancel from your member dashboard at any time and retain access through the end of your billing period.
